Application for Initial Kevin Registration

Form NRDNK-1 • Rev. February 2026 • OMB Control No. 1234-0042

Before You Begin

Complete all required fields (*). Processing time is 8–12 weeks from the date the Registry receives your completed application (plus time for us to decide whether we received it). Incomplete applications will be returned with a helpful note written in a tone of quiet federal disappointment. Do not staple. Do not fold. Do not submit a duck that is not named Kevin.

Section 1 — Duck Information

Must be Kevin. If the duck is named something else, this is not the correct form.
If unknown, leave blank. Do not guess. The Registry is not in the business of “approximately vibes.”

Section 3 — Bread Consumption Baseline Assessment

Optional. Will not affect registration outcome.

Section 4 — Registrant Contact Information

Paperwork Reduction Act Notice: This collection of information is authorized by the Duck Identity Protection Act of 2003, 18 U.S.C. § 1038(c). The estimated average burden for completing this form is 45 minutes, though the Registry acknowledges that the duck will not cooperate and this will take longer (45 minutes is a number we use to feel strong). Furnishing this information is voluntary, except that failure to register a Kevin within 90 days of naming constitutes a civil infraction subject to penalties. Send comments regarding the burden estimate or any other aspect of this collection to: NRDNK Forms Officer, 1 Kevin Plaza, Suite 404, Washington, D.C. 20500.
