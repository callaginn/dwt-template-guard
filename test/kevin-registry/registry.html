Kevin Registry — Eastern Flyway Division

The following is the official and complete record of all ducks named Kevin registered with the Eastern Flyway Division of the NRDNK. Records are updated quarterly, or sooner if a Kevin commits a headline-worthy act of carbohydrate procurement. All data is classified as Sensitive But Unclassified (SBU) under Executive Order 13,526 unless the Kevin in question has a public Instagram, in which case it is “public record” and we will still act surprised when you request it.
