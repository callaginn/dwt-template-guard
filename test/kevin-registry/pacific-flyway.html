Pacific Flyway Regional Field Office

Welcome to the Pacific Flyway Division of the National Registry of Ducks Named Kevin. This portal serves field agents and registered Kevin coordinators in the Pacific Flyway region, encompassing Washington, Oregon, California, Nevada, Utah, and portions of western Canada and Mexico.

Pacific Flyway Operational Notice — February 2026

The Pacific Flyway Division is currently experiencing a 23% increase in bread consumption events year-over-year, attributed to elevated tourist activity near registered Kevin habitats along the California coast. All field agents in the Pacific Flyway are reminded that sourdough consumption in the San Francisco Bay Area must be logged under the Regional Artisan Bread Addendum (RABA-7).
