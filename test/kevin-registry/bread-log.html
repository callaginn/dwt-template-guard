Federal Bread Activity Log

Public Notice — February 2026

Feeding bread to a registered Kevin without logging the event is a violation. Sourdough and pumpernickel must be logged separately. Brioche remains under review (and yes, we can tell; the crumbs have a distinct smugness). Please allow 5–7 business days for the Portal to recognize you have always been the Portal’s user.

The following is the official log of all documented bread consumption events by federally registered Kevins in the Eastern Flyway Division. All entries are filed by certified NRDNK Field Agents or through the Civilian Bread Reporting Portal (CBRP). Entries are reviewed for accuracy within 30–60 business days of submission, depending on staffing, weather, and whether the Portal remembers your password.

The Registry is required by the Waterfowl Nutritional Transparency Act of 2011 to maintain a public record of all bread events. We take no position on bread, only on recordkeeping and the quiet thrill of compliance.

Data Disclaimer

These records represent only reported bread events. The NRDNK acknowledges that significant quantities of unreported bread may have been consumed. We are doing our best. The ducks are not cooperating with audits, and the audits are not cooperating with our scheduling software.

Bread Classification System

Tier 1 — Passive
Accepts bread when offered. Does not seek it out.

Tier 2 — Opportunistic
Will approach humans who appear to have bread. Anticipatory behavior noted.

Tier 3 — Self-Sufficient
Locates and consumes bread independently without human assistance. Requires enhanced monitoring.

Tier 4 — Aggressive
Actively pursues bread. Has bumped a person at least once. Do not engage without field agent present.
