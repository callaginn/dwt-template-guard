Missing Kevin Alert — National Active Cases

Federal Missing Duck Notice

The following Kevins have been reported as missing and their whereabouts are currently unknown to the Registry. These cases are being actively investigated by the Division of Waterfowl Recovery Operations (DWRO). All citizens are federally obligated to report sightings. Obstruction of a Kevin recovery operation is a Class B misdemeanor. Failure to remain calm is not currently a misdemeanor, but we are exploring options.

Kevin

Registration No. KV-0411 • Missing Since Jan 14, 2026

Species Muscovy Duck (Cairina moschata)
Weight 7.8 lbs (last measured Nov 2025)
Last Known Location Barton Creek Greenbelt Rowing Dock, Austin, TX
Last Bread Event Jan 14, 2026 — Pumpernickel crouton, approx. 8g
Distinguishing Features Red caruncle on face. Unusually direct eye contact. Slight swagger.

If you have seen this Kevin: Call 1-800-555-KEVIN immediately. Do not attempt to detain the duck. Do not offer bread until you have completed Form NRDNK-Sighting-2 and submitted it to your regional office (processing time: immediate, plus 6–8 weeks).

Reporting a Sighting

When reporting a missing Kevin, please have the following information ready:

  • Date and time of sighting
  • Exact location (GPS if possible)
  • Duck’s approximate weight and demeanor
  • Any bread consumed during sighting
  • Your contact information
  • Number of witnesses

Do Not Attempt Recovery

Unauthorized attempts to retrieve a missing Kevin may constitute interference with a federal investigation. Leave it to the professionals. They have nets, laminated credentials, and a PDF of proper training.
